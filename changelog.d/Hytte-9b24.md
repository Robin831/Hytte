category: Changed
- **Pokémon scanner + search: use printed_total denominator and accept combined queries** - Adds a `printed_total` column to `pokemon_sets` (the denominator on the card face, e.g. `/142` on a Stellar Crown card), separate from the inclusive `total_cards`. The card-search endpoint now matches the denominator against `printed_total OR total_cards`, accepts combined "021 pansear" / "pansear 021" tokens (collector number AND name fragment), and the scan worker uses the denominator to disambiguate sets so Claude saying "sv1" on an sv7 card no longer mis-matches. Existing rows have `printed_total=0` until an admin re-runs `POST /api/pokemon/admin/sync` once after deploy. (Hytte-9b24)

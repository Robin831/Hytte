category: Changed
- **Paginate the workout list** - `GET /api/training/workouts` now accepts `limit` (default 25, clamped to 100) and an opaque keyset `cursor`, returning one bounded page plus a `next_cursor` (null on the final page). The Training page uses this to load workouts incrementally with a "Load more" control instead of fetching the entire history on every visit, and new-workout detection prepends fresh uploads without discarding already-loaded pages. Calls without pagination params still return the full history, so the Compare, Stride, and Lactate pages and the dashboard fitness widget are unaffected. (Hytte-0tpj)

- **i18n: Extract strings from core pages and dashboard widgets** - All hardcoded English strings in Sidebar, Home, GreetingWidget, Weather page, and all dashboard widgets (Daylight, WeatherWidget, NorwegianFunWidget, ActivityFeed, Fitness, InfraStatus, GitHubStatus, LactateSummary, QuickLinks) are now extracted into i18next translation files. Translation files for `weather` and `dashboard` namespaces are populated for `en`, `nb`, and `th` locales (English placeholders for nb/th). (Hytte-lzdx)

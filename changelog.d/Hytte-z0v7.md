category: Added
- **Kids Allowance: chore management and earnings tracking** - Introduces the `kids_allowance` feature with full data model and CRUD. Parents can create recurring chores (daily/weekly/one-time) with NOK values, post one-off extra tasks, configure bonus rules, and approve or reject completions submitted by children. Children can view their assigned chores, mark them as done, claim extra tasks, and see their weekly earnings. Completions auto-approve after 24 hours to prevent parent bottleneck. Weekly payout summaries can be generated and marked as paid. (Hytte-z0v7)

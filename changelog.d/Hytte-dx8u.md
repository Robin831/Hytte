category: Added
- **Credit card CSV import API** - Added two-phase preview/confirm endpoints (`POST /api/credit-card/import/preview` and `POST /api/credit-card/import/confirm`) for importing DNB credit card CSV exports. The parser handles Norwegian number format (comma decimal separator, unicode minus sign) and maps all 10 DNB columns. Duplicate detection skips rows matching on (transaksjonsdato + beskrivelse + beløp). Rows with "Reservert" in beskrivelse are marked `is_pending=true`; rows containing "Innbetaling" are marked `is_innbetaling=true`. After commit, `merchant_group_rules` are applied to auto-assign `group_id`. Sensitive beskrivelse data is encrypted at rest. (Hytte-dx8u)

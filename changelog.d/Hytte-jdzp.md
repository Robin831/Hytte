category: Added
- **Reward & claim storage + API** - Added `family_rewards` and `reward_claims` tables with full CRUD. Parents can create, update, delete, and manage rewards; children can browse active rewards, claim them (stars deducted atomically), and view their claim history. Parent approval/denial of claims triggers star refunds on denial. Sensitive fields (title, description, parent_note, resolution note) are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Push notifications fire when a child claims a reward (parent notified) and when a parent resolves a claim (child notified). (Hytte-jdzp)

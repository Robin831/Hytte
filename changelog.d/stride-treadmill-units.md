category: Changed
- **Treadmill-friendly interval descriptions** - Stride workout sessions now express interval main sets in both distance and time, and target pace in both min/km and km/h — e.g. "4x2000m (or 4x9min) at 4:28-4:32/km (13.2-13.4 km/h)" — so they transfer easily to a treadmill. Applies to both plan generation and chat-based plan edits.

category: Added
- **Family Chat video calls** - Extend the 1:1 voice-call WebRTC pipeline to support video. A new video-call button sits next to the phone icon in the chat header; the incoming-call overlay labels video calls separately; the active call shows a full-screen remote feed with a draggable picture-in-picture local preview. Toggles for mic, camera, switch front/back camera, hangup, and speaker live in a bottom bar. Local capture downscales to 320×240 on slow networks (slow-2g / 2g / 3g) and restores to 640×480 on 4g+. The `family_chat_calls` table grows a `kind` column (voice|video) so the conversation log can render the right icon. (Hytte-hob4)

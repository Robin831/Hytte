- **News feed** - A new "News" page (feature-gated) aggregates Norwegian RSS sources (VG, NRK, TV 2, tek.no, gamer.no, plus user-added feeds) into one stream. Hard filters hide articles by keyword, category or paywall/teaser; surviving articles are de-duplicated across sources and optionally ranked for relevance by Claude (Haiku) using a 👍/👎 taste profile. Includes timeline/columns layouts, auto-refresh with a "new articles" pill, read-state dimming, save-for-later, and a "why filtered" audit drawer. Feeds cache for 10 minutes; sensitive settings/feedback are encrypted at rest.

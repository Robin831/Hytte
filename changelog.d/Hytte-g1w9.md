- **Training load API and AI period analysis** - Adds `GET /api/training/load?weeks=N` returning weekly load records, acute/chronic load, ACR, and training status. Adds `POST /api/training/summary/analyze` which assembles a Claude prompt from weekly totals, sport distribution, load/ACR/status, and the user profile; calls Claude; caches the encrypted prompt and response in `training_summaries`; and returns structured coaching feedback with risk flags. Adds `RiskFlags []string` to `TrainingInsights` so per-workout insights also surface training risk indicators. (Hytte-g1w9)

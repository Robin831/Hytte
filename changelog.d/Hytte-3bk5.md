category: Added
- **Family Chat: SSE live-delivery stream** - Added `GET /api/familychat/conversations/{id}/stream` (gated by the new `family_chat` feature flag) that holds an `text/event-stream` connection open and pushes `message_new`, `message_deleted`, and `read_receipt` events from an in-memory pub/sub hub. Non-members get 404 so conversation existence is not leaked, and the connection sends a heartbeat every 30 seconds to defeat idle proxies. (Hytte-3bk5)
